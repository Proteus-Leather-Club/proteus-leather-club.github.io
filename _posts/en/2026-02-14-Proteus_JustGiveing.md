[Proteus Needs You!](https://www.justgiving.com/crowdfunding/proteus-leather)

Your donation to Proteus will help us:

Pay for website and mailing list costs, host events which require a deposit, produce club merchandise, produce high quality zines to uplift trans voices in the leather scene, amongst other projects.

A donation is not required for membership, but we encourage all club members who are able to donate to do so.

Money raised is spread across all international chapters, based on what is needed for various projects.
